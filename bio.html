The Story of Corey Ross

Corey was born in the small city of Rochester, NH in the spring of 1992.
After attending Spaulding High School, where he participated as a drum
major, respresentative to the school board, and president of three clubs,
Corey headed down to Medford, MA to study at Tufts University. There he
studies International Relations, with a minor in Economics, as well as
classes in computer programming. In fact, that's why this site exists!
