The Story of Corey Ross

Corey was born in the small city of Rochester, NH in the spring of 1992. After attending Spaulding High School, where he participated as a drum major, respresentative to the school board, and president of three clubs, Corey headed down to Medford, MA to study at Tufts University. There he studied International Relations, with a minor in Economics, as well as classes in computer programming. Now this site is used to showcase what he's learned and the experience he has gained.
